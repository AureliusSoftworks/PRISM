## Trigger
Darkening the active-chat BOT popout interior in light mode made colored bot labels harder to read instead of improving contrast.

## Lesson
For light-mode BOT popouts, keep the light surface language and improve readability with per-color contrast-safe bot ink, not a fixed CSS darkening mix. Avoid globally darkening the popout surface unless the text treatment is also redesigned for a dark surface.

## Applies to
`apps/web/src/app/page.module.css` `.composeBotMenu`, `.composeBotOptionGlyph`, and `.composeBotOptionName` light-mode styling.
