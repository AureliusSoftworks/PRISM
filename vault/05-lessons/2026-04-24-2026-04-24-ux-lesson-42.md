## Trigger
Bot-picker buttons disappeared after selecting a bot/item before the first message was sent.

## Lesson
Bot buttons should remain on screen after any item/tile click and only disappear when a message is sent and the conversation actually begins. Selection can still arm/highlight a bot, update the hero, and populate the compose picker, but it must not remove the button grid pre-send.

## Applies to
`apps/web/src/app/page.tsx` Chat and Sandbox empty-state bot pickers.
