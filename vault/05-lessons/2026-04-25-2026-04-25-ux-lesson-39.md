## Trigger

## Lesson
The six bot-picker stages are now: Stage 1 full cards, Stage 2 flat cards, Stage 3 larger glyph-to-container ratio, Stage 4 glyphless cards and the first stage where clicking relocates the user to that color grouping on the hue slider, Stage 5 solid swatches/pixel grid with selected-dot treatment active, and Stage 6 radial rainbow gradient as the fully abstract high-density state. In the pixel-grid stages, desktop mouse clicks should select the exact bot and also move the hue slider to that bot's color scope. Treat the Stage 6 bot ceiling as provisional until it is explicitly tuned.
